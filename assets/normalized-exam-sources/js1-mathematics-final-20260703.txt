JS1 MATHEMATICS EXAMINATION - THIRD TERM

OBJECTIVE QUESTIONS

1. A polygon with five (5) sides is called ----?
A. Pentagon
B. Hexagon
C. Heptagon
D. Decagon
ANSWER = A

2. Which of the following is not a property of an isosceles triangle?
A. Two base angles are equal
B. Two sides are equal
C. It has one line of symmetry
D. All the three sides are different.
ANSWER = D

3. Solve the equation: 3(a - 2) + 2a = 24
A. 6
B. 7
C. 5
D. 8
ANSWER = A

4. Solve: 4(y + 3) = 6(y - 1)
A. 7
B. 8
C. 9
D. 10
ANSWER = C

5. A cube has how many edges?
A. 6
B. 8
C. 12
D. 16
ANSWER = C

6. A rectangular pyramid has how many vertices?
A. 5
B. 6
C. 7
D. 8
ANSWER = A

7. A three-dimensional shape that has no edges and vertices is called ----?
A. Rectangle
B. Square
C. Triangle
D. Sphere
ANSWER = D

8. A solid figure or shape with uniform cross-sectional area is called ----?
A. Prism
B. Square
C. Triangle
D. Diamond
ANSWER = A

9. Translate the sentence into a mathematical statement: Add 7 to a number n and then multiply by 3.
A. 3(n + 7)
B. 7n + 3
C. 3n + 7
D. 3 + n × 7
