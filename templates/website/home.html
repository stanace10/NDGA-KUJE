An education shaped by faith, discipline, purpose, and the dignity of the girl child

Notre Dame Girls' Academy is owned and managed by the Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur and belongs to a wider educational tradition inspired by St. Julie Billiart, who urged educators to teach children whatever they need for life.

At NDGA, that vision appears through Catholic Gospel values, purposeful learning, pastoral care, boarding structure, and the all-round formation of every girl in our care.
