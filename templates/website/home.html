An education shaped by faith, discipline, purpose, and the dignity of the girl child

Notre Dame Girls' Academy is owned and managed by the Sisters of Notre Dame de Namur and belongs to a wider educational tradition inspired by St. Julie Billiart, who urged educators to give children what they need for life.

That vision appears in the life of the school through sound academics, Catholic values, safe boarding routine, and the all-round growth of every student.
